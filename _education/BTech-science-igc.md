excerpt: Production and Performance Analysis of Bio-diesel from Sunflower and Karanja Oils (B.Tech Main Project). This work is a viability study of bio-diesel production from Sunflower and Karanja oils. Through transesterification, bio-diesel is isolated from both oils. We modified a diesel test rig to find the load-carrying efficiency and emissions at various mixing proportions of obtained bio-diesel.
